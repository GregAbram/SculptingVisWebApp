Instructions

  1. Drag an .obj file from your computer into the Glyph Align Window.
  2. To align the glyph, you can do one or both of the following:
    • Right Click + Drag the Glyph Align Window to rotate its coordinate system.
    • Click on the Rotate + and Rotate - buttons.
  3. Left Click + Drag the Glyph Align Window to rotate its view.
  4. Left Click + Drag the Data-Driven Preview to see how the glyph will look on an example data visualization.
  5. Click Render Thumbnail to create a thumbnail image of the glyph. This image is rendered according to the view of the Glyph Align Window.
  6. When you're ready, give a Family and Class name for this glyph, then click Export Obj.
